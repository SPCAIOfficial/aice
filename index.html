About Event

AICE is a groundbreaking initiative that fosters transformative change within the Pakistani industry and society through AI technologies. By bringing together a diverse range of stakeholders including international partners, industry experts, academics, and government representatives from the Ministry of Information Technology and Telecommunication, AICE aims to spark a grand discourse on the potential of AI. At the heart of AICE's efforts lies the Lab2Market competition, which serves as a platform for innovators and entrepreneurs to showcase their AI-driven solutions and products, facilitating their transition from the laboratory to the market. Additionally, AICE organizes industrial and product expos, symposiums, seminars, and tutorials, providing invaluable opportunities for knowledge exchange, skill development, and networking within the AI ecosystem.

Organizer

Sino-Pak Center for Artificial Intelligence (SPCAI) at Pak-Austria Fachhochschule: Institute of Applied Sciences & Technology (PAF-IAST) aim to build national capacity to carry out research and development in the emerging field of artificial intelligence. SPCAI is an integrated platform where the fields of robotics, IoT, computer vision, biomedical applications and energy are represented by their dedicated labs.

  • 25+ speakers to lead grand discourse on potential of AI and success stories; Case Studies
  • Guided hands-on tutorial sessions covering AI and allied technologies
  • Pitch you projects @ Lab2Market and win exciting prizes

Lab2Market Project Registration Details

Deadline: 28th April 2024 (Revised: 3rd May 2024)

After the project is selected for the event, the group members shall need to independently register for the event using the event registration link.

Lab2Market Event consists of 3 Phases:

    Phase-I (Acceptance and Notification):
    The registered project will first undergo initial screening and be notified about their acceptance by 6th May 2024
    Phase-II (Three-Minute Pitch):
    The accepted projects from Phase-I will be invited to deliver a three-minute pitch on 8th May 2024
    Phase-III (Project Exhibition):
    The shortlisted projects after the three-minute pitch will qualify for the project exhibition at AICE on 27th - 29th May 2024.

The best projects after evaluation shall be awarded prizes in the prize distribution ceremony on 29th May 2024.
